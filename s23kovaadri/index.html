Cats in the Workforce

The misconception that cats spend their days lounging and napping has persisted for centuries. However, recent studies reveal that cats maintain highly-structured 9-to-5 work schedules, contributing significantly to global industries. Evidence of feline employment dates back to Ancient Egypt, where cats worked as rodent control specialists and were later promoted to "Deity Representatives" due to exemplary performance reviews.

Cats are compensated primarily in snacks, with performance bonuses including catnip, feather toys, or premium cardboard boxes. High-performing cats may also receive extra belly rubs (strictly on their terms) or exclusive napping spots in the sun.
