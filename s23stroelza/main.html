Dungeons and Dragons is a tabletop fantasy role-playing game (TTRPG).

The game is set in a medieval fantasy world in which multiple players form a group that is led through an adventure by one player who assumes the role of the storyteller, or Dungeon Master.

The players create a character, which partakes in the story - they choose a race (human, elf, dwarf, halfling), a class (e.g., Fighter, Rogue, Wizard), and skills for their character, and they apportion a small number of points to different attributes (Constitution, Strength, Dexterity, Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma) that determine how effective a character is in performing various actions. The probability of succeeding actions is done through dice rolls and the players' stats.
