The Simpsons

The Simpsons is an American animated sitcom created by Matt Groening for the Fox Broadcasting Company. The series is a satirical depiction of working-class life, epitomized by the Simpson family, which consists of Homer, Marge, Bart, Lisa, and Maggie.

Since its debut on December 17, 1989, *The Simpsons* has broadcast over 700 episodes, making it the longest-running American animated series and sitcom.

Each of the main characters has a unique personality that contributes to the show's humor and satire.

Homer Simpson

Marge Simpson

Marge is characterized by her iconic blue hair and her dedication to the family.

Bart Simpson

Bart is the mischievous 10-year-old son with catchphrases like "Ay, caramba!"

Lisa Simpson

Lisa is the intelligent and socially aware daughter, passionate about issues like the environment.
