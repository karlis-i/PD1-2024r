German Car Brands Overview

Germany has a legacy of producing some of the world’s most admired car brands, renowned for their engineering, design, and innovation. Explore the profiles of iconic brands and discover what sets each apart.

BMW

Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, commonly known as BMW, is famous for its performance and luxury vehicles. It continues to set standards in automotive engineering. Parent Company: BMW Group

Audi

Audi represents the epitome of style and cutting-edge technology. Known for its iconic quattro all-wheel-drive, Audi remains a leader in the premium car market. Parent Company: Volkswagen Group

Mercedes-Benz

Mercedes-Benz is celebrated for luxury, innovation, and safety. The brand continues to push boundaries in automotive technology. Parent Company: Mercedes-Benz Group

Volkswagen

Volkswagen, or "People’s Car," focuses on practicality and accessibility. It has been a favorite for those seeking reliable and versatile cars. Parent Company: Volkswagen Group
