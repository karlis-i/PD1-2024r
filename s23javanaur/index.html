About South Park

South Park is a satirical animated series created by Trey Parker and Matt Stone. Known for its humor and irreverent take on current events, the show features the adventures of four boys in a small Colorado town.

Meet the Characters

Stan Marsh

Stan is the grounded and level-headed member of the group, often acting as the leader in difficult situations.

Kyle Broflovski

Kyle is the intelligent and moral member of the group, often serving as the voice of reason.

Eric Cartman

Cartman is loud, manipulative, and self-centered, providing comic relief and driving the plot with his antics.

Kenny McCormick

Kenny is the mysterious, brave, and loyal friend, famous for his muffled speech and frequent dramatic moments.
