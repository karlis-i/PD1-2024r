Ecco2k - E

Ecco2k - AAA Powerline. (here I'd put a song if i owned the rights to it)

The album E by Ecco2k is an outstanding cult classic adored by fans all over the world for its ethereal, otherworldly sounds and vocals. This album falls into the category of "outsider music", with it's glossy, drainy aesthetic paired with the very serious message of mental struggles of being a black man in sweden, that gets pretty dark if the listener pays close attention to the lyrics.

This is the debut solo album released by Ecco in 2019 and so far it's the only one he has put out, whilst fans can't get enough of it Ecco is keeping radio silence about his upcoming releases, most likely focusing on perfecting his art.

The album E, could be considered a self titled, the tracklist consists of 12 officially released songs that are the following

The album might not be everyone's cup of tea, but those who enjoy it, truly love it. With this album Ecco2k helped solidify his unique "drainy" sound that you can not find anywhere else, thus leaving the fans craving for more. Ecco knows this and leans into the persona in many ways, such as his style and mannerisms

So what's next for Ecco? Nobody truly knows, we just have to be patient and look forward to his next album.

⛓Drain Gang⛓

Drain Gang is an artist collective that Ecco2k is a part of, the group conists of similiaraly skilled and unique artists such as Bladee, Thaiboy Digital, Whitearmor and Gud. They are close friends and work together on many things and ventures, chances are if you've heard of one of them you know all of them, since it is a very tight-night community. Each of the members bring something to the table that elevates all of their collective being. They even have their own ideology that takes very huge inspirations from Taoism, while they dont say it out right the lyrics and symbolisms speak for themselves

Drain gang collective

Ecco2k

Ecco2k, also known as Zak Arogundade Gaterud, is a Swedish artist, producer, and designer known for his genre-blurring music that blends elements of experimental pop, electronic, and cloud rap.His ethereal vocals, futuristic production, and unique visual aesthetics have made him a distinctive figure in the underground music scene. Ecco2k's 2019 debut album E received critical acclaim for its innovative sound and introspective lyrics, solidifying his reputation as a boundary-pushing artist.

Bladee

Bladee, also known as Benjamin Reichwald, is a Swedish musician, fashion designer, and visual artist. As a leading member of the Drain Gang collective, he has gained recognition for his distinctive blend of ethereal, auto-tuned vocals with dreamy, melancholic production, often combining elements of cloud rap, electronic, and experimental pop. Bladee’s introspective lyrics and otherworldly soundscapes, along with his visually striking artwork and fashion, have made him a key figure in the underground and internet music scenes.

Thaiboy Digital

Thaiboy Digital is an artist from Bangkok, Thailand, known for his eclectic mix of cloud rap, electronic, and experimental music. Raised in Sweden, he became a prominent member of the Drain Gang collective, alongside Bladee and Ecco2k. His melodic flows, auto-tuned vocals, and genre-defying production have earned him a dedicated following within the underground music scene. Thaiboy Digital's unique sound combines vibrant, futuristic beats with introspective and often emotional lyrics. His 2019 album Legendary Member solidified his place as a key figure in the global alternative music landscape.

Whitearmor

Whitearmor is a Swedish producer and key member of the Drain Gang collective, known for crafting the distinct, atmospheric sound that defines much of the group's music. His production style blends ethereal melodies with experimental, cloud-like beats, often incorporating elements of ambient, trap, and electronic music. Whitearmor's ability to create dreamy, emotive soundscapes has made him a sought-after collaborator, not only within Drain Gang but also across the broader underground and alternative music scenes. His work on albums like Bladee's 333 and Ecco2k's E has cemented his reputation as a pivotal figure in shaping the group's signature sound.

Gud

Gud, also known as Carl-Mikael Göran Berlander, is a Swedish producer and DJ best known for his work with the rap collective Sad Boys, alongside Yung Lean. His production style blends melancholic, atmospheric melodies with trap-influenced beats, helping to define the emotional, dreamlike sound of the Sad Boys movement. Gud's innovative use of synths, vocal samples, and electronic elements has made him a standout figure in the underground music scene. His contributions to Yung Lean’s early albums, such as Unknown Memory, played a crucial role in shaping the global influence of the cloud rap genre.
